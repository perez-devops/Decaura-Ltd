Our Process
01

Initial Consultation

We begin by listening — understanding your vision, lifestyle, requirements and ambitions for the space before a single line is drawn.

02

Project Alignment

We define the full scope, timeline, and budget. Every parameter is agreed upon clearly, ensuring the entire project is built on a foundation of mutual understanding.

03

Concept & Design Development

Our team develops a considered design concept — spatial layouts, material palettes, lighting schemes and furniture selections — curated to reflect the brief with precision.

04

Detailed Planning

Technical drawings, procurement schedules and contractor coordination are finalised. Every detail is resolved before work begins, eliminating ambiguity on site.

05

Execution & Oversight

We manage the build and fit-out with continuous on-site oversight — monitoring quality, timeline adherence, and workmanship at every stage of delivery.

06

Installation & Refinement

Furniture, fixtures and finishes are installed with white-glove precision. Final styling and adjustments ensure the space is resolved exactly as envisioned.

07

Completion & Handover

The completed space is handed over with full documentation. We conduct a final walkthrough together, ensuring every element meets the standard we set at the outset.
