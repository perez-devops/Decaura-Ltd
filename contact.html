Frequently Asked Questions

What is it like to work with Decaura?

Working with Decaura is a structured and considered experience. From the outset, every stage is clearly guided, ensuring a seamless process where design, execution and communication are handled with precision and care.

How involved will I need to be throughout the project?

We take a highly managed approach, allowing you to remain as involved or as hands-off as you prefer. Our role is to ensure the process feels effortless, while keeping you informed at every key stage.

How do you ensure the final result meets expectations?

Every project is guided by a clearly defined brief, detailed planning and continuous oversight. This ensures that all decisions remain aligned with the agreed vision, resulting in a space that is both considered and accurately delivered.

How are budgets managed throughout the project?

We establish a clear financial framework early in the process and maintain careful oversight throughout. This ensures that design intent and investment remain aligned, with full transparency at every stage.

What level of quality can I expect?

We work to a consistently high standard across every aspect of the project, from materials and finishes to execution and detailing, ensuring a refined and enduring result.

How do you handle project timelines?

We provide clear timelines from the outset and manage every stage of delivery to ensure milestones are met. Our structured approach allows us to maintain momentum and deliver with predictability.

Do you manage contractors and suppliers?

Yes. All aspects of execution are coordinated and overseen by our team, ensuring consistency, quality, and alignment with the design.

Can Decaura work on projects outside of Nigeria?

Yes. Our approach is not limited by location, and we are able to take on projects across different regions where required.

Blending international design sensibility with local Nigerian market expertise to create unparalleled luxury spaces.
