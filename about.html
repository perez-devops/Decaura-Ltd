About Decaura.

Luxury Interior Design & Project Management studio defined
by a considered approach to space.

Who we are

More than design.
A discipline.

Decaura is a luxury interior design and project management studio, built on the belief that great spaces are not decorated — they are architected.

We work with discerning clients who understand that the environment you occupy shapes how you think, feel, and perform. Our role is to ensure that environment is exactly what it should be.

Founded on a commitment to precision, we combine international design standards with local knowledge and a client-first approach to deliver spaces that are both extraordinary and enduring.

Meet our Founder

Marie Takongou

Marie Takongou is the Founder of Decaura, with a perspective informed by extensive international exposure across some of the world's most architecturally and culturally significant design centres — including London, Paris, Dubai, Italy and China — where she developed a nuanced understanding of design at the highest level.

Across these environments, she cultivated an appreciation for materiality, proportion and the quiet discipline that defines truly exceptional interiors. Guided by this experience, Marie returned to Nigeria with a clear intention to introduce a more elevated standard of interior design and project management — one defined not only by aesthetic excellence, but by structure, professionalism and a seamless client experience.

She recognised a gap within the local landscape where refined design, disciplined execution and attentive client service are rarely delivered in equal measure. Decaura was established to bridge that gap, bringing together global perspective and local context to create spaces that are both distinctive and enduring.

Her work is characterised by clarity, restraint and an exceptional eye for detail. Each project is approached with intention, ensuring every element is purposeful and every space stands apart with quiet confidence. The result is a body of work that is not only visually compelling, but unmistakably considered.

Under her direction, Decaura represents a new standard — where design is executed with precision, service is delivered with care, and every project reflects a level of refinement that is both rare and assured.

The Decaura Foundation

The Decaura Foundation is the philanthropic arm of Decaura, established to extend the principles of design and refinement beyond private projects and into the wider community.

The foundation works to create meaningful and measurable impact by supporting individuals and communities through access to knowledge, improved environments and pathways to long-term opportunity.

Our vision is that individuals and communities are equipped with the tools, resources and environments they need to thrive, contribute and shape their own futures.

Our work is focused across four key impact areas:

01

Design Education

expanding access to design knowledge, training, and industry exposure

02

Community Spaces

improving shared environments to support wellbeing, connection and everyday experience

03

Youth Development

providing structured support, mentorship and opportunities for growth

04

Journey to Employment

enabling pathways to skills development, career readiness and sustainable employment

Through these focus areas, the foundation supports initiatives that equip individuals with practical skills, improve living and community environments and contribute to long-term social and economic development.

The Decaura Foundation works in partnership with organisations, communities and stakeholders to deliver programmes that are both carefully considered and sustainably structured. Each initiative is approached with clear objectives, defined outcomes and a commitment to measurable impact.

Funding for the foundation is supported through Decaura and its wider network, ensuring that resources are directed towards initiatives that align with its long-term vision and areas of focus.

As the foundation grows, its aim is to expand its reach across Nigeria and beyond; supporting individuals and communities at scale, while maintaining the same level of care, discipline and intention that defines Decaura.

Through the Decaura Foundation, design becomes a tool for lasting impact extending beyond aesthetics to influence opportunity, environment and future potential.
